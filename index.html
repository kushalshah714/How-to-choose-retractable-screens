How much sun do you
want to block?

If your main issue is too much heat or glare, you need a shade mesh, not a bug screen. Simply pick how much sun you want to block and how much you still want to see through.

Maximum shade

Block the heat. Keep your privacy.

This mesh blocks most of the sun and heat. You can still see out from inside, but people outside cannot easily see in. Your space stays noticeably cooler all day.

Best for: west or south facing patios that get full afternoon sun
Balanced — most popular

Good mix of shade and view.

This is what most people choose. It cuts the heat and glare a lot while still giving you a clear view outside and letting good light in. Most homeowners are very happy here.

Best for: most outdoor living areas, covered patios, anywhere you want comfort without giving up the view
Open view

Keep the view. Take the edge off.

You can see clearly through this mesh almost as if nothing is there. It takes the harsh edge off the sun without darkening your space. Best for spots where the view is everything.

Best for: beautiful views you do not want to lose, enclosed porches, light shade needs
